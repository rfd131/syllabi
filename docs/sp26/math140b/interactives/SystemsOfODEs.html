Systems of ODEs — phase portraits

Explore two-dimensional autonomous systems x′ = F(x,y), y′ = G(x,y). For linear systems, eigenvalues of the coefficient matrix classify the portrait (node, saddle, spiral, center) and its stability. For nonlinear systems, equilibria sit at the intersections of the nullclines; the Jacobian at each equilibrium gives a local linearization. Click the phase plane to launch a trajectory.
