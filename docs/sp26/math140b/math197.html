What is Math 197?

Prior to the late drop date (Sunday, April 12, 2026), you have the option to change enrollment from MATH 140B (4 credits) to Math 197, a 2-credit course.

Passing grades in both Math 197 and the follow-on two-credit course Math 198 (Topics in Calculus with Applications to Biology) are together equivalent to a passing grade in the 4-credit MATH 140B.
